# Darcy System Requirements

## Power

* 110V AC or 220V AC with a local plug adapter
* Darcy uses 18 Watts of power to operate
* You can run Darcy on a battery or any power source that can provide 5 Volts at 3.5 Amps or can provide 18 Watts through a 110V or 220V inverter

## Connectivity

* 802.11ac WiFi that can run on 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz bands

  OR

* Hardwired Ethernet

## Environment

* Dry space \(Devkit is not rated for outdoor use\)
* 3-30' of working distance \([See field of view guidelines](../edgeworx-for-developers/quickstart/field-of-view-guidelines.md)\)
