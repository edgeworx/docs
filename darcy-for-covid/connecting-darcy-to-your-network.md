# Connecting Darcy to your network

## Wifi Setup

1. Connect the power switch and cable to Darcy and press the button
2. Wait for the LED on the front of Darcy to turn blue
3. On your computer or latop, go to available wifi networks and choose wifi network that looks like “**DarcySetup123**” and enter password“**darcysetup123**”
4. Using your web browser, visit “**http://darcy.local:12345**”
5. Enter your wifi network name and password, click “Add Network”
6. Click reboot button and give Darcy a few minutes to join your network and get settled \(3-5 minutes\)

## Ethernet Setup

1. Plug in Ethernet cable
2. Connect the power switch and cable to Darcy and press the button
3. Give Darcy a few minutes to join your network and get settled \(3-5 minutes\)
