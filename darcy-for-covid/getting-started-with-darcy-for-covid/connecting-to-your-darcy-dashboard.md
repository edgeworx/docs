# Connecting to your Darcy Dashboard

## Connecting to Darcy Dashboard

1. Make sure your computer, tablet or phone is on the same network as Darcy
2. Use a web browser \(Chrome, Firefox, Safari\) to visit “[http://darcy.local](http://darcy.local/)” 
   1. Click the live view icon to see what Darcy sees
   2. Click the report view icon to see stats from the last day, week or month  

## Darcy Pass

1. Create a Darcy Pass \(used with checkin mode\) by visiting “[www.meetdarcy.app](http://www.meetdarcy.app/)” or search your app store for "Darcy Pass"
