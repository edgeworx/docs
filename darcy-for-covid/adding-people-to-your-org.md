# Adding people to your org

## Add a group of individuals with the same email domain to your org

1. Send your default organization email domain \(EX: "@yourcompany.org"\) to [Support@meetdarcy.com](mailto:mailto:support@meetdarcy.com)

## Add individuals via specific email address

1. Download the "[darcy\_new\_accounts.xlsx](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5f9b30034cedfd0016107e89/attachments/6025a9470a2dae5b58faeef8/darcy_new_accounts.xlsx)" Excel template
2. Complete the template including organization members first name, last name and email address
3. Email the completed file to [Support@meetdarcy.com](mailto:mailto:support@meetdarcy.com)
4. If the file is received before noon MST \(GMT-7\), it will be processed by the following morning. If received later, it will be processed in 48 hours or less

## For young children or other visitors who will have Darcy Passes created by parents or guardians on their behalf

1. Download the "[darcy\_new\_accounts.xlsx](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5f9b30034cedfd0016107e89/attachments/6025a9470a2dae5b58faeef8/darcy_new_accounts.xlsx)" Excel template
2. Complete the template including the parent or guardian first name, last name and email address
3. Email the completed file to [Support@meetdarcy.com](mailto:mailto:support@meetdarcy.com)
4. If the file is received before noon MST \(GMT-7\), it will be processed by the following morning. If received later, it will be processed in 48 hours or less
5. The parent/guardian will create their account using their own name and email and then [add their children or dependents through the Darcy Pass app](https://darcy-support.helpscoutdocs.com/article/9-getting-started-using-darcy-pass).
