# Ensuring Darcy visitors are successful

## Make it easy for Darcy to see your face

* Take your hat off or put it high enough that your forehead shows
* Pull your bangs up so Darcy can see your forehead
* Remove your glasses
* Pull your mask up or down a little so Darcy can see your eyes and neck

## Make your Darcy Pass easy for Darcy to read

* Bring up your Darcy Pass on your phone before you approach the Darcy kiosk
* Turn up the brightness on your phone if it is dim
* Hold your phone so that Darcy can see your phone and your face at the same time
* Watch for glare on your phone screen and turn it slightly if necessary to remove glare and give Darcy a clear view of the QR code
