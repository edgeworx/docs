# Ensuring Darcy visitors are successful

## Make it easy for Darcy to see your face

* Take your hat off or put it high enough that your forehead shows
* Pull your bangs up so Darcy can see your forehead
* Remove your glasses
* Pull your mask up or down a little so Darcy can see your eyes and neck

## Make your Darcy Pass easy for Darcy to read

* Download the Darcy Pass app from home \(in case reception at your destination is poor\)
* Bring up your Darcy Pass on your phone before you approach the Darcy kiosk
* Turn up the brightness on your phone if it is dim
* Hold your phone so that Darcy can see your phone and your face at the same time
* Watch for glare on your phone screen and turn it slightly if necessary to remove glare and give Darcy a clear view of the QR code

## More helpful tips

* Make sure the lighting is ok! Not too dark or bright.
* Hats to be pulled up or taken off to prevent face coverage or shadows.
* Long hair shouldn't cover face and may require that it be put behind the ears. 
* Make sure faces are towards the center of the screen \(the temp reader has a hard time if the face is at the top of the screen\).
* Wait for Darcy to run through her checking of temperature, mask, and checking them as complete before showing the QR code.
* If a phone with the code is in her "face", it will create issues with reading the person's face. As mentioned above, let her get the first two checks done then show the QR code to her. 
* On screen there are white boxes which indicate where the code should be. If it is hard to see, we can change the color to something more noticeable. 
* Take your time! Darcy is pretty quick but make sure you are checked in and that the following person isn't right on their heels. Wait for the "Hello" on screen before starting your scan after another person.
