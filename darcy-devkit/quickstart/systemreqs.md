# Darcy System Requirements

## Power

* 110V AC or 220V AC with a local plug adapter
* Darcy uses 18 Watts of power to operate
* 

## Connectivity

* 802.11ac WiFi that can run on 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz bands

  OR

* Hardwired Ethernet

## Environment

* Dry space \(Devkit is not rated for outdoor use\)
* 3-30' of working distance \([See field of view guidelines](field-of-view-guidelines.md)\)
