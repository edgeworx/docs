# Getting Started with Darcy Devkit

## 1. Power up Darcy

1. Connect the included power supply to Darcy
2. Connect to a standard 110V outlet \(or 220V with a local adapter\)
3. Wait for the light to turn blue. This may take several minutes.

## 2. Get Darcy on your network

### Ethernet setup

1. If you are using a wired Ethernet connection, just plug the Ethernet cable into Darcy's port. 

### or Wifi Setup

1. You will need to connect from your laptop to Darcy to configure the wifi credentials.
2. Look for "**DarcySetup**_**\_**_" in your list of available wifi networks. The blank part will be filled in with a random number, e.g. "DarcySetup489".
3. The wifi password for this network is "**`darcysetup123`**"
4. Open your web browser and go to [`http://192.168.50.5:12345`](http://192.168.50.5:12345)
5. Input your wifi network name or SSID and your wifi credentials and click "**Connect to Wifi**" button \(You can add more than one\). Both the WiFi network name and password are case sensitive.
6. Click the Reboot button and wait a few minutes for Darcy to reboot.

## 3. See Darcy in action

1. Point your web browser to `http://YOUR-DARCY-HOSTNAME:3456/live-feed` to see the live feed. Your Darcy name is the device "hostname" and can be found in the welcome email you received.
2. SSH in to your Darcy from anywhere by going to [https://cloud.deviceplane.com](https://cloud.deviceplane.com) and signing in with the credentials you were given in your welcome email.
3. Monitor and manage your Darcy by going to the management URL provided in your welcome email.
